About Us

Our History

In the years following its creation, Eco-Sud has led various fights, demonstrating its ability to act as a watchdog by mobilizing and taking action. The association has developed two main forms of action: one based on advocacy and awareness, and the other based on education, research and conservation projects. In 2010, the NGO created a programme called Blue Lagoon, positioning themselves as a provider of solutions based on scientific research, whilst in close collaboration with the local community. Eco-Sud does not only aim to alert the public but strives to create a better future with a committed society. The association has been and remains the initiator of important discussions and actions, raising awareness for the public, proposing alternative solutions and drawing attention to the irreversible environmental consequences of the Mauritian non-responsible modernisation.
